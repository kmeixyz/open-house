About the event

Our community is
special to us

HECC's Adult Education Department would like to formally invite you to our first ever Open House. Adult education programming provides numeracy, literacy, English language skills, digital literacy, work readiness, soft skills, high school equivalency, and numerous wrap-around services.

Howard & Evanston Community Center serves hundreds of adult learners every year — providing a safe space for students to build community here in the United States.

What to expect

Six reasons to come out

International Craft Fair

Browse handmade crafts and artwork celebrating the diverse cultures of our student community.

Student Cookbook

Check out our collaborative cookbook featuring authentic recipes contributed by our adult learners.

Meet our instructors and discover what programs are available — literacy, ESL, GED prep, and more.

Build Community

Connect with fellow learners, families, neighbors, and community supporters in a warm, welcoming space.

Music & Food

Enjoy live music, delicious food, and creative crafts that reflect the rich tapestry of our community.

Have Fun!

Most importantly — come ready to laugh, learn, and celebrate everything our community has built together.
